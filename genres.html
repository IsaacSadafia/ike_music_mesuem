Genres of Music

A selection of influential genres across time and cultures.

Classical

Orchestras, chamber music, symphonies, and operas.

Jazz

Improvisation, swing, bebop, and modern fusion styles.

Blues

Expressive vocals and the 12‑bar form influencing rock & soul.

Rock

Guitar-driven sound—from classic rock to alternative.

Pop

Melodic hooks, polished production, global appeal.

Hip‑Hop

MCing, DJing, sampling, and beats culture.

Electronic

Synths, drum machines, house, techno, ambient.

Reggae

Off‑beat rhythms, ska roots, and dub innovations.

R&B / Soul

Vocal-centric grooves blending gospel and blues.

Gospel

Faith-driven vocal harmony and call-and-response.

Country

Storytelling with acoustic and slide guitar textures.

Folk

Tradition-forward songs carried by communities.

Afrobeats

Contemporary West African pop with global rhythms.

Highlife

Ghanaian dance music—guitars, horns, and syncopation.

Latin

Salsa, bossa nova, reggaetón—rich rhythmic diversity.

Metal

Heavy riffs, intricate drums, and dramatic vocals.
